K2 Black Panther

Posted September 15, 2023

The K2 Black Panther, a South Korean main battle tank, boasts advanced technology, composite armor, and exceptional firepower for modern warfare. Mass production began in 2013, and the first K2s were deployed with the South Korean armed forces in July 2014. It integrates advanced features like composite armor, a powerful 120mm smoothbore gun, and a sophisticated fire control system.
